OCTOBER 8 2021 | 3 - 4 PM

OCTOBER 8, 2021 | 3 - 4 PM

Through this activity, students under the ABM strand will be oriented and familiarized with their fellow students and teachers. ABM alumni will be invited as well, to provide their own insights and advice. Teachers will also have a chance to speak, by doing this can provide their own insight, advice and explain the expectations for the school year ahead. Orienting the students will allow them to have an insight into the ABM strand and school year to follow, whilst also allowing them to prepare ahead of time. By having ABM alumni and teachers involved, the students may gain hefty amounts of knowledge, and ask further questions in an open-forum Q&A. Students are expected to use what they learn from this event to help prepare them for the upcoming school year, and feel more comfortable openly communicating with teachers and peers.
