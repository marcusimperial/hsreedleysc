SPECIAL ELECTIONS

September 1 2021

At the end of each School Year, students from Middle School 6 up to Senior High School vote for their Grade Level Representatives. Unfortunately for HUMSS, abstain got the most votes while STEM had a tie. 

The Special Elections will be handled by the CO​MELEC which is an ad-hoc volunteer committee of non – HUMSS and STEM students. 

Through this activity, aspiring student leaders for the position of HUMSS and STEM Representatives will go through the process of elections. The winning candidate will work with other elected Student Council Officers for SY 2021 – 2022. They are expected to learn and gain leadership experience as they serve the school through various student–initiated and student–centered activities that promote leadership and excellence.

NEW SMILES 2021

August 20 2021

In line with one of the four core values of Reedley--which is family, the Reedley International School Student Council, together with the high school volunteers, will welcome the new students through an acquaintance party entitled “New Smiles”. The party will be composed of getting to know you activities, games, and presentations.
